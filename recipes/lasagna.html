Lasagna Recipe

Delicious Lasagna!

Flat pasta, tomato sauce, ricotta, mozzarella, and your choice of protein. What's not to love? This simple lasagna recipe is guaranteed to hit the spot and banish your cravings.

Ingredients:

  • 1lb of ground beef (or your choice of protein).
  • 1 (15 oz.) can of tomato sauce.
  • 1 box of lasagna noodles.
  • 2 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese.
  • 1 cup of ricotta cheese.

Steps:

  1. Bring large pot of water to a boil. Introduce pasta to water and cook for 8 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 Degrees F.
  3. In a large pan over medium heat, brown beef and then drain.
  4. Mix beef and tomato sauce.
  5. In large baking pan, alternate layers of noodles, sauce mixture and cheese; Fill the pan
  6. Bake for 30 minutes.Enjoy!
