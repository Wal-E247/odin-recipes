Scrambled Egg Recipe

Scrambled Eggs!

Scrambled eggs are quick, easy and nutritious. Ready in minutes and packed with the nutrients you need to get through the day. Always a great dish, scrambled eggs are even better when you are short on time and need something fast.

Ingredients:

  • 1 or more eggs.
  • 1 tablespoon of butter.
  • salt and pepper.

Steps:

  1. Add eggs, salt and pepper to mixing bowl; mix thoroughly.
  2. Heat pan over high heat.
  3. Add butter to hot pan.
  4. After butter melts, add eggs.
  5. Stir egg mixture until desired texture is reached.
